Product Description
Activities that you and your students are sure to love because they are actively engaged as they practice addition basic facts to discover the picture hiding on the page!

Students will solve basic addition facts to 20 as they try to discover the hidden picture on the page.

Activities include:

Add 1 Girl Mermaid
Add 2 Muddy Pig
Add 3 Boy in a Tube
Add 4 Boy Mermaid
Add 5 Girl with Sunglasses
Add 6 Boy with Flower
Add 7 Girl with Watermelon
Add 8 Owl on a Branch
Add 9 Fish in a Bowl
Add 10 Bear in the Bushes
